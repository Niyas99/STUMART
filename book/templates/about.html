ABOUT US


The software named “STU MART” that we introduce for the Students. The development and implementation of this software designed to facilitate the buying and selling of study materials for students. The proposed software aims to create a seamless and efficient marketplace where students can exchange textbooks, lecture notes, guides, and other educational resources. Now a days after the current class the study materials will become as useless and there is a less chance to sell the materials to other students that who actually needs the material. By using this software the students can sell the books after their use. And those who needs the materials that they can buy through this platform. Here we provide a platform for the students to sell and buy the materials. The primary objective of this software is to create a digital marketplace where students can easily buy and sell study materials, including textbooks, notes, research papers, and supplementary resources.
